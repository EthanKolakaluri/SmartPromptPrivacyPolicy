Privacy Policy

Thank you for using SmartPrompt. We value your privacy and want to be transparent about how we collect and use your data.

What data we collect

SmartPrompt collects the input text you provide when interacting with AI services such as ChatGPT, Claude, and Lovable. This input data is necessary for us to generate optimized prompts and deliver the best user experience.

How your data is used

The user input is sent securely to our backend servers, where it is processed using APIs from ChatGPT, Claude, and Lovable. We do not store your input data longer than necessary to provide the service.

Third-party services

We rely on third-party AI providers—OpenAI (ChatGPT), Anthropic (Claude), and Lovable—to process your prompts. These providers may have their own privacy policies, which we encourage you to review:

  • OpenAI Privacy Policy
  • Anthropic Privacy Policy
  • Lovable Privacy Policy

Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data in transit and at rest, using industry-standard encryption and secure authentication.

Your choices

You can choose not to provide input data, though this will limit SmartPrompt’s functionality. If you have any questions or concerns about your data, please contact us at privacy@smartprompt.example.
